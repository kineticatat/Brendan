Musicican. Dancer. Martial Artist. Traveler. Circus.

Live. Laugh. Love

Travel

Brendan loves traveling whether that is locally or internationally. He has been to 27 states in the USA and does not plan to stop there. Overseas, he has been to Mexico, Canada, Japan, Italy, and South Korea. He loves to experience new cultures and hopes to travel more in the near future, especailly New Zealand.

Music

From a very young age, Brendan started immersing himself into the music world. He started taking piano lessons at the age of 7 and soon later started learning the cello, violin, voice, guitar, and flute. He has performed with various orchestras and choirs throughout his life. In 2014, he performed in Carnegie Hall as well as several venues in Italy such as the St. Peter's Basilica with the Wright State University's Collegiate Chorale. In 2016, he participated in Ohio's divison of Music Teachers National Association chamber music competetion with Wright State University's piano trio, Troika, and they had received alternate. Brendan graduated from Wright State University with a Bachleor of Music in vocal performance and is currently pursuing a Master of Music in music therapy at Illinois State University.

Circus

Brendan was recently introduced to the art of circus and has been a part Illinois State University's Gamma Phi Circus since September of 2018. In 2019, he was seen performing teeterboard, acro sport, and dance. In 2020, He can be seen performing in perch pole, bamboo, jump, teterboard, and dance. Recently, he was seen performing at the 2019 Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade in New York City with Gamma Phi Circus, performing alongside Emmy, Tony and Grammy Award-winning actor, singer, director, composer, and playwright Billy Porter.
